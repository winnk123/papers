   (B) 若 $\alpha \perp \beta$, $m \parallel \alpha$, 则 $m \parallel \beta$
   (C) 若 $m \parallel \alpha$, $m \parallel \beta$, $\alpha \cap \beta = n$, 则 $m \parallel n$
   (D) 若 $m \parallel \alpha$, $m \parallel \beta$, $\alpha \cap \beta = n$, 则 $m \perp n$
【答案：C】
